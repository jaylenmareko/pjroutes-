Strategy: Start in lower-demand markets to sharpen the pitch. Work up to premium markets once the pitch is dialed.

## Outreach Sequence (per Marquett)

1. Scrape FAA Part 135 operators by state → get name + email
2. Send mass email to Tier 1 states
3. Call non-responders within 48 hours
4. Log results, refine pitch
5. Move to Tier 2, repeat
